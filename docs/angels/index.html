Asure Network Angels Program

Asure Network is a layer 2 scaling network that enables fast, easy and secure transactions for not only payment transactions, but also generalized smart contracts. Founded by German insurance and technology experts, the network enables everyone to quickly build, operate, and use highly scalable business applications through innovations in multi-chain scaling techniques and incentive-aligned crypto economics mechanisms.

Join us as a Asure Network Angel.

Hello and welcome to the Asure Angels Program. If you are wondering what that is, it means you are in the right place.

Why Angels Program?

Generally we are a non-profit organisation. We are developing decentralized network for Social Security use-cases like pension/insurance/basic income systems on Blockchain.

Who are Asure Angels?

Asure Angels are people who understand the vision of the Asure Foundation and who'd like to support it in some ways. Our goal is to help people improve their lives by improving the systems that matter the most for people, meaning the Social Security systems.

As an Asure Network Angel you are working directly with the Asure team.

What do angels have to do?

We expect our Angels to come and tell us what their skills are, what their experience is and how they can help the project. If you are interested in joining Asure Angels program, you can register here below.

One Angel of ours translates our articles to Japanese and also has a Japanese Twitter for Asure. This is just an example of a possible support.

Will I be paid?

First of all, it is important to understand that the Asure Angels are volunteers who are interested in supporting the project and want to change things the way we do for the better.

All Asure Angels will be paid with tokens, but if your primary objective is to earn money, it is probably not the best way to do it. There are projects oriented at quick profits, maybe they can even pay fiat money. We don't do that. We develop our network for the community. That's why we are looking for supporters.
